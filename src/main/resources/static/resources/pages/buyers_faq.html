How does the buyer contact the seller?

BizRealWorth.com uses a unique simplify and streamline the process of buying or selling a business. The matching system allows buyers to indicate their interest in a business listing, and it gives sellers the opportunity to screen prospective buyers before sharing further information.

When a buyer is interested and would like to further inquire about a business, the buyer can call BRW or use Contact Us link. The seller will then receive a notification that a buyer is interested. The seller then has the option to either accept or ignore the request.

If a match request is accepted by a seller, a match is formed between the buyer and seller. Only then will they be able to begin a secure, live conversation, exchange further information about the business and each other.
