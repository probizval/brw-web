How do you sign up on Bizrealworth.com to save postings, searches, posts, notes, etc?

Go to the Register link (or the log in link) at the top right of the page on Bizrealworth.com. Add your information on the form and you will be emailed a confirmation link to verify your online registration. Just click on the link in the email and your Bizrealworth.com Account will be activated. When you are logged into Bizrealworth.com you can save your search criteria, save postings and posts, on all content on the site, If you have any problems contact Bizrealworth.com Customer Support.

How to Create a Successful Profile?

Creating and maintaining an accurate and up-to-date profile on Bizrealworth.com can go a long way when it comes to having your match request accepted. Your profile can only be viewed by a seller once you’ve sent your match request, so any information you share will be kept private until that point.

Ensure that your profile is completely filled out. This helps the sellers of the business(es) you are interested in purchasing learn more about who you are and what you do. Include relevant information about your background and education, as well as your previous professional experience.

How does the buyer contact the seller?

Bizrealworth.com uses a unique simplify and streamline the process of buying or selling a business. The matching system allows buyers to indicate their interest in a business listing, and it gives sellers the opportunity to screen prospective buyers before sharing further information.

When a buyer is interested and would like to further inquire about a business, the buyer will request by clicking the star icon on the business listing page. The seller will then receive a notification that a buyer is interested. The seller then has the option to either accept or ignore the request.

If a match request is accepted by a seller, a match is formed between the buyer and seller. Only then will they be able to begin a secure, live conversation, exchange further information about the business and each other.
